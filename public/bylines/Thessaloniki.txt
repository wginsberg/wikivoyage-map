Thessaloniki (Greek: Θεσσαλονίκη, Albanian, Turkish: Selanik, Serbian, Bulgarian, Macedonian: Солун, Solun, Judaeo-Spanish: סאלוניקו / Saloniko, Romanian: Salonic, Aromanian: Sãrunã, French: Salonique) is the capital of the administrative region of Central Macedonia and the whole historical region of Macedonia, Greece, and is, at about one million inhabitants (2011), the second largest city in the country.
